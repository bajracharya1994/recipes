Ingredients:

2 Eggs
1tspn milk
1tspn vegetable oil
Salt

Procedure:

Heat vegetable oil in Pan. While it's heating, break the eggs into a bowl, mix the milk and stir until mixed well. Put some salt for taste and other spices.
Pour the mix to the pan and let it cook. You can add black pepper if you like, it gives a different taste to your omelette.
